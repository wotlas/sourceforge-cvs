Maps

There are three kind of maps in wotlas :

World maps. Your player is seen as a red dot, places and towns where you can go are displayed in blue. The movements on world maps are very slow but if you start a long trip and quit the game, your character will be still travelling on the server. So if you re-connect later you'll find that your character has reached his/her destination...

Note also that you can not see other players on world maps but you can still chat with them (this behaviour may disappear in a next version of wotlas).

Town maps. Your player is seen as a red dot, places and buildings where you can go are displayed in blue. Movements on town maps are not saved when you quit the game : you re-start at your latest position.

Note also that you can not see other players on town maps but you can still chat with them.

To quit a town map follow the roads !

Building maps. It's the only maps where you can see other players. Just click on the screen to move your player to the place you want. These maps can be huge, so they are usually divided into rooms. You only see players that are in the same room as you or in a near room. The name of the current room is displayed in the upper-right of the game window. As you may have noticed you can only speak to players that are in the same room as you. Of course, if you shout, players that are in near rooms will hear.

Players

You can see your player and other players on building maps. To get some information on a player click on him/her, it will display :

his / her full name on the screen and other information in the Information Panel on the right of the screen. You'll also see the last time you eventually met him / her.

his / her aura. The aura reflects the community/rank of a character.

if he / she is offline, an eventual 'Away' message... It's a message each player can set in the Away panel to tell something to other players when offline.

For more information read our FAQ.

Doors

Doors can be opened / closed by clicking on them when you are near them. You can also click on a door to get near it and then open / close it.
