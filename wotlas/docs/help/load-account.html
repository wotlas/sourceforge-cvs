How do I load a previously created account ?

Well, that's simple... On the main Wotlas screen you'll find the list of your accounts. Just select one of them and click Load.

You'll then be asked to enter your password and if it succeeds your game data will be loaded.

If the server connection fails it probably means that the server is not running. You should :

try to connect later.

take a look at the News window that is displayed when you start the game. There might be some information about your wotlas server.

If you still can't connect to your server, contact us on our sourceforge site : http://sf.net/projects/wotlas. Go to the support section or leave a message in a forum.
